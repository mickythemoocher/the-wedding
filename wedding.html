The Kitchen Scene
Paul - The Groom
Andy - The Best Man

Andy

- The Bedroom Scene -
Kay: Ohh No! it doesn't fit

Phylis: Of course it does dear. Hmm let me just pull this down a bit

Liz: It's a beautiful dress Kay

Kay: ( A little annoyed) I know it is a beautiful dress – just not on me.

Phylis: You look gorgeous in it darling

Kay: But it doesn't fit! It did when I bought it but it doesn't now

Phylis: Hmm Maybe a little bit tight but it does fit

Kay: If it's a little bit tight then it doesn't fit

Liz: No time to change it now Kay, the wedding is in an hour

Kay: No it isn't

Phylis: It is dear, just under an hour in fact

Kay: No it isn't

Liz: It is Kay, honestly

Kay: No it isn't – because I am not going

Phylis: Don't be silly Kay, you have to go. It is your wedding.

Liz: Yes, people get very disappointed at weddings when the bride doesn't tun up

Kay: I don't care. I am not, not, not going.

Phylis: You are, are, are. I am not going to have all those people laughing at me because the bride has changed her mind

Kay: What about them laughing at me? I will be walking down the aisle like a robot in a dress I can't move in.

Phylis: Don't be so ridiculous. You will look nothing like a robot

Liz: Your mum's right Kay you won't look at all like a robot. More like you have your legs tied together

Kay: Ahhh. You see mum?

Phylis: Please shut up Liz that didn't help at all.

Liz: Hmm, I suppose not

Kay: I am taking it off and not going

Liz: What about Paul? You can't let him down on his wedding day. He will be devastated

Kay: Well he will just have to be devastated because I am not going.

Phylis: You really can't do that to him dear. Leave him standing at the alter like that

Kay: I'll message him

Liz: Message him????

Phyis: You cannot possible send him a message

Liz: No!

Phylis: What are you going to say? Sorry I can't make it today something has turned up?

Kay: Something like that

Phylis: I forbid it. Paul is a lovely man and you can't treat him like that

Liz: You don't know how lucky you are Kay. Nobody wants to marry me

Kay: That's because you are too choosy

Liz: No I'm not

Kay: Yes you are. What about that man from The other side of town. You wouldn't marry him

Liz: No, because he smelled of fish

Kay: Not his fault – he worked in a fish shop. See? You are choosy. Now help me get this dress off.

Phylis: No! Definitely not – you are going

- The Kitchen Scene -
Paul:It's no good. I just can't get these trousers buttoned up

Andy: Give it a good tug

Paul: I have given it a good tug and it just won't meet

Trevor: I can't believe you Paul. How could you not have tried them on till today?

Paul: I kept meaning too but just never got around to it

Andy: You must have put a lot of weight on Paul

Paul: Yes I suppose I have

Trevor: You must have done if they fitted when you bought them

Paul: What am I going to do? I can't go to my wedding like this.

Andy: You have to go mate. You can't let Kay down on her wedding day.

Paul: But I can't go to the church like this

Trevor: Maybe you could wear a belt

Paul: I am wearing a belt

Trevor: I can't see it

Paul: No, because my belly is hanging over it

Andy: Yes you have definitely put on a bit weight Paul

Trevor: Don't you have another suit you can wear Paul?

Paul: Only the clown suit I wore at that fancy dress party

Trevor: Well you can't wear that

Paul: Why not? I'll look like a clown anyway.

Andy: I'd lend you one of mine but the wouldn't fit, I am a bit smaller than you

Trevor: And mine would look too big for you

Andy: Maybe you cold hold something in front of you

Paul: Like what? A crate of beer? That would look good in the church

Andy: Only trying to help

Paul: There is only one thing for it I will have to cancel, I can't go looking like this

Trevor: You can't cancel Paul. Kay would be devastated. You can't stand her up on her wedding day.

Paul: I'll make her look a fool anyway if I turn up looking like this

Andy: So what are you going to do?

Paul: I'll text her

Trevor: Don't be stupid, you can't text your future wife on her wedding day to say you are not coming

Paul: Well what else can I do? There is no possible way I can go like this.

Andy: Your dad is right mate. You can't send a text like that

Trevor: Surely you have another pair of trousers that would fit. Would it really matter if it wasn't a suit?

Paul: I am a car mechanic dad, all I have got is jeans

Andy: No, you can't really go in jeans

Paul: I am sure Kay will understand why I can't be there

Trevor: I have been married for 37 years and one thing I can tell you for certainty Paul is that she will not understand

Paul; Perhaps I could pretend to be injured

Trevor: You will be injured if you don't turn up to that wedding. And not just by Kay

Andy: Very true, her mum and dad are going to be after you too.

Paul: Oh what a fix I am in. If I don't turn up everyone will hate me and if I do turn up everyone will hate because I look like a tramp.

Andy: On a scale from one to ten I think it may be better to turn up looking like a tramp

Trevor: Yes, so do I

Paul: No. I just can't do it. I'd never be able to face them again

Andy: I don't really see you have any choice mate

Trevor: No, nor do I

Paul: This is supposed to be the happiest day of my life and it has turned out so miserable

Trevor: Come on son, let's make a move.

Paul: Just a minute – I have had an idea.

- The Bedroom Scene -
Phylis: Oh please don't take the dress off Kay I am sure we can think of something

Kay: No, it's coming off

Liz: Such a shame it's a beautiful dress

Kay: It is, but it doesn't fit and I refuse to get married in it

Liz: I'd love to try it

Kay: Here, take it – keep it for all I care

Liz: Oooh can I?

Phylis: NO Liz, we have to get Kay to the church

Kay: I told you I am not going

Phylis: Please Kay

Kay: No

Phylis: I'll buy you a new car

Kay: A new car?

Phylis: Hmm - Well maybe not a new car – you can have my old one

Kay: Still no

Liz: I am going to try it on

Phylis: No Liz please don't. Give it back to Kay

Kay: I don't want it

Liz: I am going to try it then

Kay: You are smaller than me so will probably fit you fine

Liz: Can you zip me up please?

Kay: Sure

Phylis: Oh this is terrible. Terrible

Liz: Wow it feels wonderful

Kay: It looks really good on you Liz. Fits you perfectly

Phylis: Kay please, please put it back on

Kay: Absolutely not

Liz: I love it so much I just don't want to take it off

Phlylis: But you must Liz

Liz: I suppose so

Kay: Ahh Just a minute. I have an idea

- The Kitchen Scene -
Trevor: It had better be a good one because you need a very good idea

Paul: Andy, You put the suit on. It would fit you

Andy: What!! Why??

Trevor: What on Earth are you up to Paul? How is that going to help?

Paul: I promise you it will. Let me take it off and I'll tell you how.

Andy: This isn't making any sense to me

Trevor: Or me

Paul: I am sure it will work

Andy: I can't see how.

Paul: Andy, you have to do me a really huge favour

Andy: Of course. You have always been my best mate. What do you want me to do

Paul: So firstly put this suit on

Trevor: I am seriously worried about you Paul. You seem to have lost your mind

Paul: No I haven't, I have found the solution

Andy: I can't see how me wearing your suit is a solution

Trevor: I can't either

Paul: It will be I promise

Andy: Well look, At least I can get the trousers buttoned up

Paul: It fits you like a glove

Trevor: I must say the suit does look good on you Andy but I still can't see how this can help

Paul: I'll tell you how it can help – Andy is going to take my place

Andy: (Horrified) I can't do that!

Trevor: No he can't Paul. It is a ridiculous idea

Paul: No it's not. It will work

Andy: I know I said I would do anything for you Paul but marrying your girlfriend is going a bit too far

Paul: You wouldn't really marry her. You would pretend to be me.

Andy: I am sure Kay is going to notice that she is marrying the wrong bloke

Paul: It is ok, I will message her and tell her what is happening

Trevor: I wish you luck with that, she will go absolutely crazy

Paul: Not if I explain it right. We will still be married, it's just that Andy will be the one going through the ceremony, with my name

Trevor: I am sure that is illegal

Andy: And what about all the guests? They are expecting you to get married to Kay, not me

Paul: We'll explain it all to them later

Trevor: It will take a lot of explaining

Paul: Please say you will do it Paul. Please!

Andy: I'll probably regret it but ok, I'll do it

Paul: Great! Thanks mate – I owe you one, I am going to text Kay right now, tell her what's happening

Trevor: This is crazy, crazy, crazy. It'll never work

- The Bedroom Scene -
Phylis: You don't need an idea Kay. You just need to put that dress back on

Kay: I told you, I'm not going to do it

Liz: What's the plan then?

Kay:You are going in my place Liz

Liz: WHAT!!

Phylis: Kay, That is the most ridiculous thing I have ever heard

Kay: It will work. I know it will

Liz: But I can't marry Paul, he is your boyfriend not mine

Kay: You wouldn't marry him, you would pretend to be me

Liz: But I look nothing like you, I am sure Paul would notice.

Kay: Of course he will know it is not me. I will text him to tell him what is happening. At the alter you say my name and when you sign the register sign it as me. Paul and I will be married but we won't be at the church

Phylis: Oh my goodness, I can't believe this. It is immoral, illegal and also insane

Kay: Relax Mum, it will be ok. Just a minute, I have a text from Paul

- The Church Scene -
Vicar: Do you Paul take Kay to be your lawful wedded wife?

(there is a short silence)

Vicar: Ahem! Do you Paul take Kay to be your lawful wedded wife?

(Another short silence)

Andy: Oh, oh sorry. You are talking to me aren't you?

Vicar: Well yes.

Andy: Sorry, I forgot

Vicar: You forgot??

Andy: Yes sorry, I forgot I am Paul

Vicar: Forgot your name?

Andy: Err yes, sorry, it happens sometimes

Vicar: Sometimes you forget your own name?

Andy: Errr not often. Just when I am stressed. Getting married is stressful, particularly this one

Vicar: Alright, alright. Just say I do

Andy. Yes, I do

Vicar: Thank you. And do you Kay take Paul to be your lawful wedded husband,(whispers) I would think carefully about this if I were you

Liz: I do

Vicar: Hmm. I now pronounce you man and wife. You you may kiss the bride

(Sound of kissing)

Andy: (Whispering) Ohhh Liz I have always fancied you so much but was too shy to say

Liz: (Whispering) Really? I always fancied you too, for years but thought you were not interested in me.

Andy: Oh you silly girl, I have been in love with you since we were kids.

Liz: I can hardly believe this, I have always loved you too.

Andy: Liz (short pause) Will you marry me.

Liz: Again? (A short laugh) Dear Andy, of course I will. But on our wedding day, let's make sure we have clothes that fit
